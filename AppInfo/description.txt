- Gestione archivio musica

- Gestione di playlist musicali
- Possibilità di caricare video e musica da qualsiasi computer collegato alla stessa rete Wireless a cui è collegato anche il telefono
- Riproduzione musica in background

Muvitool is an App for iOS, iPad and iPhone devices, completely free and without the presence of advertising, which allows you to manage video and audio archives.
The main features are:
- Video archive management
- Music Store Manager
- Saving Videos in the Roll Room
- Uploading videos from the roll room
- Extract audio from downloaded videos in m4a format, setting: title , artist and cover
- Manage musical playlists
- Possibility to upload video and music from any computer connected to the same wireless network to which the phone is connected
- Play music in the background
